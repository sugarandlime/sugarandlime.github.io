This is Sugar & Lime

Oh sister ! Thy will be done, but I will see no tears.
Remember, In the bushes where we hid treasures.
I still smell those mangoes you left, no scale of love to measure.
A part of me still doused in the sweetness of your hand which fed my stomach.
You build us with childish oaths,
A promise to climb mountains and sail the mighty boats.

Our Childhood

At grandmother's home we flew like birds,
Fell in dirt, you picked me up, and filled humanity in this filthy nerd.
Remember,the bed time stories we shared, and how we travelled the forest gump,
Small canals or big wells, we took the jump.
Actually the childhood we spent never died,
For me it's in my heart, for you a mellow tart,
I picture it as the finest of all art.

Our dreams

How dare, I should perish before you cry.
Oh sister ! Thy will be done, but I will see no tears.
Remember, All the honey we stole from the bears den,
All the farms we rolled and the solace hemmed.
The dress you wore, and the trick you rolled
Believe me, I have seen plenty of butterflies but you are the rarest and pure.

I can go as far as possible for you!

If millions of star try to shed,
I will steal them all to prepare your bed.
The bed which entagles you with its shine,
I may not be perfect brother but you have been perfect all the time.
Last time I checked, I sensed your pain.
But, you tried to resist and never shared the same.

You are strongest and beautiful of all

I have seen brave men and true knight,
But all obliterated by time, only you being my ideal 9.
You are manifestation of our mother,
I have a whole world through your eyes to see.
Those eyes which I would never let sense fear.
Oh sister ! Thy will be done, but I will see no tears,
Though, one except out of cheers.

Let's end it before my tears roll

Cold nights and our silly fights, for you my sister I will take all those spears.
Smile and smile like soft clouds white and clear,
Or be the crane and fly with your dreams everywhere.
You are like city of 'olathe', a beautiful mention.
And I write this poem my sister, out of affection,
Not for a perfect creation,Still It will be a perfect windchyme,For I will fill the spaces.
Your love will be the breeze providing perpetual rhyme.
